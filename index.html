three small ways to feel close

pairing

built for exactly two people

01

open coople, get your code

a single 6-character code is generated just for the two of you — no account, no sign-up form.

02

send it to your person

text it, say it out loud, write it on a napkin — however you'd like to get it to them.

03

they enter it, you're paired

that's it. from then on, anything either of you sends finds its way to the other's lock screen.

need a hand?

if something's not working, or your pairing code isn't landing, write to us directly — we read every message ourselves.

is my data private?

coople only ever connects exactly two people at a time. there are no public profiles, no friend lists, and nothing you send is visible to anyone outside your pair.

what if i lose my pairing?

email us at hello@coople.app with both names on the account and we'll help you get reconnected — it usually takes a few minutes.

does it work on android?

not yet. coople is built natively around iOS lock screen widgets, so for now it's iPhone only.

i found a bug

thank you — truly. send a quick note to hello@coople.app describing what happened and which iPhone you're on, and we'll dig in.

how do i delete my data?

email hello@coople.app from the address associated with your pairing and we'll remove your records within a few days.
