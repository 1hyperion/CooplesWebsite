privacy policy

effective June 20, 2026

Short version: Coople only ever connects two people. We don't sell data, we don't show ads, and we don't know your name unless you email us. Everything below explains exactly what's collected and why.

Who we are

Coople is made by Astro Developments. If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is handled, you can reach us at hello@coople.app.

Information we collect

Account and pairing information. When you open Coople, Apple's CloudKit assigns your device an anonymous record identifier tied to your iCloud account. We use this identifier — not your name or email — to link you to your partner once you pair. We also store the date you paired and the 6-character invite code used to do so.

Content you share. Photos, drawings, status updates, and the animal avatar you choose are stored so they can be delivered to your paired partner's lock screen. This content is visible only to the one partner you're paired with — never to us, the public, or any other user.

Device and delivery information. We store a push notification token so your partner's device can be woken to display new content. We may also receive basic crash and performance diagnostics (device model, iOS version) to fix bugs — this never includes the content of what you've shared.

Information we don't collect

  • Your name or email address (unless you choose to email support)
  • Your location
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Browsing history or activity outside the app
  • Advertising identifiers — Coople shows no ads and does no ad tracking

How we use your information

Information is used exclusively to operate the core function of Coople: delivering what you send to the one partner you're paired with, rendering your lock screen widget, and sending the push notification that triggers that update. We do not use your content or pairing data for analytics, advertising, or any purpose beyond running the service.

Where your data lives

All content and pairing data is stored on Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, within Apple's iCloud ecosystem. Apple's own privacy practices govern the underlying infrastructure layer; you can read about those at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or other third parties. Your shared content is only ever visible to the one partner you're paired with.

How long we keep your data

Your pairing and content are retained for as long as your account remains paired and active. If you'd like everything deleted, email hello@coople.app from the address associated with your pairing and we'll remove your records, typically within a few days.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the information we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@coople.app. If you're in the European Economic Area, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children's privacy

Coople is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used Coople and shared information with us, contact hello@coople.app and we'll remove it.

Security

Content is transmitted and stored using Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, which applies encryption in transit and at rest, and ties access to your iCloud account. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we rely on the same infrastructure Apple uses across its own apps.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: hello@coople.app
